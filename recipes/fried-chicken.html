    • Crispy Fried Chicken Recipe

    Simple juicy fried chicken marinated with only 5 ingredients.

    Ingredients

    • Flour
    • Chicken legs
    • Salt
    • Pepper
    • Cream Bottle
    • Garlic powder

    Steps

    1. Wash the chicken legs.
    2. Pour a decent amount of flour in a bowl.
    3. Pour the bottle of cream in a separate bowl.
    4. Add the chicken legs in the bowl of cream and let it soak overnight or your choice of time.
    5. Put a good amount of salt, pepper and garlic powder in the bowl of flour.
    6. Mix the flour in the bowl to combine the salt, pepper and garlic powder.
    7. Pour a large amount of oil in a frying pan.
    8. Add the chicken in the bowl of flour with salt, pepper and garlic powder.
    9. Mix the chicken well and once the oil is hot enough carefully put the chicken in the frying pan.
    10. Once it's done let it dry on a paper towel and rest in a room temperature.
