Cheesy Pizza Recipe

Delicious pizza recipe with diffent kinds of cheese.

Ingredients

  • Dough
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Cheddar Cheese
  • Mozzarella Cheese
  • Cream Cheese

Steps

  1. Firstly flatten the dough.
  2. Add tomato sauce on the dough.
  3. Put a fair amount of cheddar cheese.
  4. Next put a decent number of cream cheese.
  5. Add a good amount of mozzarella cheese.
  6. Sprinkle the parmesan cheese on top.
  7. Finally put it in the oven and wait for it to bake.
