    • Big Juicy Burger Recipe

    A big juicy burger with cheese,tomato, lettuce and secret sauce.

    Ingredients

    • Burger buns
    • Butter
    • Cheese
    • Tomato
    • Burger patty
    • Lettuce
    • Ketchup, mayo and mustard

    Steps

    1. Toast the bun and melt a decent amount of butter.
    2. Brush a good amount of butter in both buns.
    3. Season the patty with salt and pepper.
    4. Next grill the patty until its medium rare.
    5. Add the cheese on top when the patty is nearly cooked.
    6. Slice the tomato in two equal size and wash the lettuce.
    7. Combine and pour a fair amount of ketchup, mayo and mustard in a small bowl.
    8. Finally assemble the burger.
